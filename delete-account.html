Account Deletion & Data Removal

At qbcalculator, we value your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and remove all associated information, you can do so by following the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

You do not need to be logged into the app to request deletion. Simply send an email from your registered email address to our support team:

Support Email: qbcalculator@gmail.com

Email Requirements:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request - qbcalculator
  • Body: Please state clearly that you wish to delete your account and all associated data stored in our Firebase database.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon processing your request, the following data will be permanently and irreversibly removed from our servers:

  • Authentication Data: Your email address and Firebase login credentials.
  • Calculation History: All saved construction and renovation calculations.
  • User Preferences: Any customized settings or cloud-synced configurations.

Processing Time

Requests are typically processed within 7 business days. Once the deletion is complete, you will receive a confirmation email. Please note that once data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.
